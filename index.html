About

Human by design, in design.

Provenance is a responsible technology organization founded by Ryan Vasquez: an AI governance consultant, ethicist, and writer working at the intersection of emerging technology, policy, and human-centered systems.

Every day, powerful tools are being adopted that are capable of real good, and deployed faster than the questions around them are being asked. Who is at risk? Who is accountable? How do we build responsibly in a landscape that keeps shifting? Provenance exists to answer those questions. Not to slow innovation down, but to make sure it moves forward on solid ground.

Focus AI governance, regulatory compliance, AI fluency education, responsible implementation of new technologies
Clients Public and private sector, including medical clinics, nonprofits, technology companies, and more
Education MA Ethics & Society (AI & Emerging Tech), Fordham University; AIGP Certification, IAPP
Based United States (available globally, proficient in international regulatory systems)
