What it does

Day-by-day planning

Organize tasks by day with a clean, focused view. Drag to reorder, move between days, and see your week at a glance.

Projects & tags

Group tasks by project. Tag anything and find it instantly. Completed tasks are archived by project so nothing gets lost.

Google Calendar sync

Connect your Google Calendar and TaskBloc automatically schedules your tasks into available time slots every morning.

Weekly summary

Review everything you accomplished each week, broken down by project and by day. Your work, accounted for.

Data & Privacy

Your data is
yours alone.

TaskBloc is built by Provenance — a responsible technology organization grounded in ethics and transparency. We apply those same principles to how we handle your data.

Here is exactly what TaskBloc collects and why:

  • Task names, notes, tags, and dates — to power your task list
  • Google account identity — to give you a private, personal workspace
  • Google Calendar access (optional) — to schedule tasks into your calendar on your behalf
  • Basic device info — for compatibility and bug fixes only

We do not sell your data. We do not run ads. We do not share your information with third parties beyond what is required to operate the service.
