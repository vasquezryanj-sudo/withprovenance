Responsible Governance Framework

Five pillars of responsible AI.

Every Provenance engagement is structured around five pillars. These form the backbone of governance assessment, framework design, and ongoing review.

Transparency
How clearly an organization discloses AI use, data flows, and decision-making processes to stakeholders.
Fairness
Whether AI systems produce equitable outcomes across demographics, with bias monitoring and adversarial testing in place.
Accountability
The mechanisms that ensure human oversight, audit trails, and clear responsibility for AI-assisted decisions.
Privacy
Data protection practices, consent protocols, regulatory compliance, and the treatment of sensitive information in AI pipelines.
Robustness
The reliability, monitoring, incident response, and change control protocols that keep AI systems safe in production.

Cornerstone Product

Your governance starting point,
in five minutes.

CompassAI is a free, open-access diagnostic tool built by Provenance. It gives any organization a structured starting point for AI governance, no matter their size, sector, or stage of AI adoption.

Ten questions. Five pillars.
A profile built for your context.

CompassAI maps your organization's current AI posture against five pillars of responsible governance: transparency, fairness, accountability, privacy, and robustness. The result is a custom governance profile, not a generic checklist, tailored to your industry, your people, and the way you actually use AI.

It's designed to be the first conversation, not the last. CompassAI surfaces the questions worth asking and the gaps worth closing, giving organizations a clear, honest foundation before building policy, adopting tools, or engaging a consultant.
